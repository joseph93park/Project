Spring

Spring is the perfect time to plant your tomatoes!

They need a lot of sunlight and do well in warm soil, so it is best to plant in late spring

They also need a lot of space, so please do be sure to plant them spaced far apart.

Add lots of compost and fertilizer to get great plump tomatoes in just a few weeks!.

Summer

Summer is the perfect time to plant sweet potatoes

They grow well even in the heat and grow in as little as 90 days. They are low maintenence once the vines being to spread across the ground!

As for flowers, Dainty white Yarrow flowers will add a nice touch of elegance to the Garden.

They do well with loads of sunlight and well-draining soil.

Fall

Mariachi 'Salsa' Helenium boasts a beautiful fall color when they bloom

They are a must have flower to make your fall flowerbed absoltely stand out.

It's a full-sun perenial that starts blooming in late summer and blooms through fall.

Its blossoms open in traditional fall hues of orange, red and gold!.

Winter

There is a big misconception that winter is barren and that we can't plant anything during this season

Crocus, Kaffir Lilies, and Scilla make up a beautiful flowerbed.

It may also be a surprise that persimmons grow well in the winter.

They are a hardy fruit that can handle temperatures as low as 25 degrees Fahrenheit.
